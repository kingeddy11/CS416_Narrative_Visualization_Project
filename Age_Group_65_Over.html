Educational Attainment in the US, California, Pasadena (CA) by Age Demographic

This interactive visualization compares educational attainment in the United States, California, and Pasadena (CA). More specifically, we analyze the percentage of the population in each region with at least a high school diploma and the percentage of the population with at least a bachelor's degree across four different age groups. Additionally, you can choose between examining educational attainment in 2022 and 2021 for these regions. We would like to observe whether Pasadena has a higher educational attainment level compared to statewide and nationwide levels.
